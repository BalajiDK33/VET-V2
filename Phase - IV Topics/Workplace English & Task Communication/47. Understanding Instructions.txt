Understanding Instructions
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "If I understand correctly, I need to [task] by [deadline]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your manager gives you a task to prepare a report. How do you confirm you understand the instruction?
2. Your team leader asks you to complete a presentation by Friday. How do you confirm the deadline?
3. Your supervisor tells you to send an email to a client today. How do you confirm the task?
4. Your manager asks you to update the sales data by noon. How do you repeat the instruction to confirm?
5. Your colleague gives you instructions to arrange a meeting. How do you confirm you understand?

Examples:

"If I understand correctly, I need to prepare the report by Monday."
"If I understand correctly, I need to send the client email by end of day."
"If I understand correctly, I need to update the sales data by noon."
"If I understand correctly, I need to arrange the meeting by tomorrow morning."
