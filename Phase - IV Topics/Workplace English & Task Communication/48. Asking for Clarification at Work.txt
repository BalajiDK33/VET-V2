Asking for Clarification at Work
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Could you please clarify [instruction] so that I can [action]?"

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your manager gives you an unclear task. How do you ask for clarification?
2. Your team leader explains a process but you did not understand one step. How do you ask for clarification?
3. Your supervisor sends an email with unclear instructions. How do you ask for clarification?
4. A colleague explains a new system but you are confused. How do you ask for clarification?
5. Your manager gives feedback that is not clear. How do you ask for clarification?

Examples:

"Could you please clarify the approval process so that I can submit the form correctly?"
"Could you please clarify the project scope so that I can assign the tasks correctly?"
"Could you please clarify the deadline so that I can plan my work accordingly?"
"Could you please clarify the reporting format so that I can complete the document correctly?"
