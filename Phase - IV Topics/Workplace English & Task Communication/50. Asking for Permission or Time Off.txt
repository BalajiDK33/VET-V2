Asking for Permission or Time Off
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I would like to request [leave/permission] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. You have a medical appointment tomorrow. How do you ask your manager for the day off?
2. You need to leave early today for a family function. How do you ask for permission?
3. You want to work from home next week. How do you request permission from your manager?
4. You need two days off for a personal commitment. How do you request leave?
5. You want to attend an external training session. How do you ask your manager for permission?

Examples:

"I would like to request a day off because I have a medical appointment tomorrow."
"I would like to request permission to leave early because I have a family function."
"I would like to request work from home permission because I have a personal commitment."
"I would like to request two days of leave because I have a family event to attend."
