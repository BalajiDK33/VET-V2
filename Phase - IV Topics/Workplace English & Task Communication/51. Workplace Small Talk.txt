Workplace Small Talk
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "It has been [adjective] lately — how are you managing [situation]?"

Roleplay Questions:

1. You meet a colleague in the break room. How do you start a small talk conversation?
2. You see a team member looking stressed before a meeting. How do you check in with them?
3. You are waiting with a colleague before a call starts. How do you make small talk?
4. A coworker just returned from leave. How do you start a friendly conversation?
5. You meet your manager in the hallway. How do you make a brief friendly comment?

Examples:

"It has been quite hectic lately — how are you managing the new project deadlines?"
"It has been very busy lately — how are you managing the extra workload?"
"It has been quite challenging lately — how are you managing the new system changes?"
"It has been rather intense lately — how are you managing the back-to-back meetings?"
