Reporting Task Status
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I have completed [task] and I am now moving on to [next step]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your manager asks for an update on your work. How do you report your task status?
2. Your team leader wants to know how far you are with the project. How do you give an update?
3. Your supervisor asks if you have finished your assignment. How do you respond?
4. Your colleague asks what you are currently working on. How do you update them?
5. Your manager checks in during a meeting. How do you report your progress?

Examples:

"I have completed the data entry and I am now moving on to the final review."
"I have completed the client report and I am now moving on to the presentation slides."
"I have completed the market research and I am now moving on to the analysis."
"I have completed the training module and I am now moving on to the assessment."
