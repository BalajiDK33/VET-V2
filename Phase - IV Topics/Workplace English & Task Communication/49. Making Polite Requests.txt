Making Polite Requests
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Would it be possible for you to [action] by [time]?"

Roleplay Questions:

1. You need your colleague to share a file. How do you make a polite request?
2. You need your manager to review your report. How do you ask politely?
3. You need a team member to complete their task. How do you make a polite request?
4. You need your supervisor to approve your plan. How do you ask politely?
5. You need a colleague to reschedule a meeting. How do you make a polite request?

Examples:

"Would it be possible for you to share the file by end of day?"
"Would it be possible for you to review my report by tomorrow morning?"
"Would it be possible for you to complete your task by Friday?"
"Would it be possible for you to approve my plan by noon?"
