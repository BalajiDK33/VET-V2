Introducing Oneself at Work
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "My name is [name] and I have joined as [role]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. You are joining as a sales associate. How do you introduce yourself to your team?
2. You have joined as a data analyst. How do you introduce yourself to a colleague?
3. You are a marketing executive on your first day. How do you introduce yourself?
4. You have joined as an HR executive. How do you introduce yourself to a senior employee?
5. You are a customer support associate. How do you introduce yourself to your new team?

Examples:

"My name is Priya and I have joined as a marketing executive."
"My name is Rahul and I have joined as a data analyst."
"My name is Ananya and I have joined as a customer support associate."
"My name is Arjun and I have joined as a junior software developer."
