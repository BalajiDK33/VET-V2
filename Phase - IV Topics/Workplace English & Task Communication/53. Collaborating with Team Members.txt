Collaborating with Team Members
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "If we [suggestion], we can achieve [outcome] more effectively."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your team is struggling to meet a deadline. How do you suggest a way to work together?
2. Your project has too many tasks for one person. How do you propose dividing the work?
3. Your team members are not communicating well. How do you suggest a solution?
4. Your group needs to improve its productivity. How do you propose a collaborative approach?
5. Your team is working on a presentation together. How do you suggest a way to collaborate?

Examples:

"If we divide the tasks equally, we can achieve the project deadline more effectively."
"If we schedule a daily check-in, we can achieve better team coordination more effectively."
"If we share updates regularly, we can achieve smoother progress more effectively."
"If we assign clear roles to each member, we can achieve the project goals more effectively."
