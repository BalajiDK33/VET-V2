Recommending Items and Comparing Products
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I would recommend [item] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your friend asks which laptop to buy for work. How do you give a recommendation?
2. A colleague asks which phone is better for daily use. How do you recommend one?
3. Your family member asks which streaming app to subscribe to. How do you suggest one?
4. A friend asks which restaurant to visit in your city. How do you make a recommendation?
5. Your colleague asks which productivity tool to use for managing tasks. How do you recommend one?

Examples:

"I would recommend the noise-cancelling headphones because they block all background distractions."
"I would recommend this laptop because it has a long battery life and is easy to carry."
"I would recommend this restaurant because the food is fresh and the service is excellent."
"I would recommend this productivity app because it helps you organise tasks and set reminders easily."
