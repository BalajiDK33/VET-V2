Planning and Decision-Making in Groups
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I think the best approach is [decision] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your team cannot agree on a project plan. How do you suggest the best approach?
2. Your group is deciding where to have a team lunch. How do you share your suggestion?
3. Your team is choosing between two project ideas. How do you help make a decision?
4. Your group is planning a company event. How do you propose the best approach?
5. Your team is discussing how to divide work for a deadline. How do you suggest a plan?

Examples:

"I think the best approach is to assign one lead per task because it avoids confusion."
"I think the best approach is to meet once a week because it keeps everyone on the same page."
"I think the best approach is to vote on the final idea because it gives everyone a fair say."
"I think the best approach is to start with the most urgent tasks because it helps us meet the deadline."
