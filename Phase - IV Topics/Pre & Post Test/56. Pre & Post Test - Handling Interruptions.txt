Handling Interruptions
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "I understand [point], and if I may just add [response]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "I understand your concern, and if I may just add that we have a solution ready."
2. "I understand the deadline is tight, and if I may just add that we can split the work."
3. "I understand your point about the budget, and if I may just add that we have a lower-cost option."
4. "I understand the urgency, and if I may just add that I have already started on it."
5. "I understand the issue you raised, and if I may just add that we are already aware of it."
6. "I understand your feedback, and if I may just add that the team has made the corrections."
7. "I understand the priority has changed, and if I may just add that I will adjust my plan accordingly."
8. "I understand the client is unhappy, and if I may just add that we have a recovery plan in place."
9. "I understand the process needs improvement, and if I may just add that I have some suggestions."
10. "I understand there is a delay, and if I may just add that we are working to resolve it immediately."

Post Test Questions:

1. "I understand the scope has increased, and if I may just add that we may need more time."
2. "I understand you are concerned about quality, and if I may just add that we have added a review step."
3. "I understand the meeting is running long, and if I may just add one final point before we close."
4. "I understand the instructions have changed, and if I may just add that I will update my task list."
5. "I understand there is a budget constraint, and if I may just add that I can explore cost-saving options."
6. "I understand the design needs revision, and if I may just add that I can have it done by tomorrow."
7. "I understand the decision has been made, and if I may just add a suggestion for future reference."
8. "I understand the report is incomplete, and if I may just add that I need one more hour to finish."
9. "I understand your expectation, and if I may just add that I have been working towards the same goal."
10. "I understand the team has concerns, and if I may just add that I am open to discussing solutions."
