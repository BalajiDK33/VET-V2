Talking about Chores and Everyday Activities
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Every [time], I make it a habit to [activity]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Every morning, I make it a habit to plan my tasks for the day."
2. "Every weekend, I make it a habit to clean and organise my workspace."
3. "Every evening, I make it a habit to prepare my clothes and bag for the next day."
4. "Every night, I make it a habit to review what I accomplished during the day."
5. "Every Sunday, I make it a habit to do the laundry and tidy my room."
6. "Every morning, I make it a habit to drink a glass of water before breakfast."
7. "Every week, I make it a habit to call my parents and check in with them."
8. "Every evening, I make it a habit to take a short walk after dinner."
9. "Every month, I make it a habit to review my savings and expenses."
10. "Every Friday, I make it a habit to clean the kitchen and restock the groceries."

Post Test Questions:

1. "Every morning, I make it a habit to read the news for fifteen minutes."
2. "Every Sunday, I make it a habit to prepare meals for the entire week."
3. "Every evening, I make it a habit to journal about my day."
4. "Every week, I make it a habit to water the plants at home."
5. "Every night, I make it a habit to keep my phone away before sleeping."
6. "Every Saturday, I make it a habit to spend time with family without any screens."
7. "Every morning, I make it a habit to stretch and do light exercises."
8. "Every month, I make it a habit to donate unused items to charity."
9. "Every evening, I make it a habit to update my to-do list for the next day."
10. "Every week, I make it a habit to read a few pages of a book before bed."
