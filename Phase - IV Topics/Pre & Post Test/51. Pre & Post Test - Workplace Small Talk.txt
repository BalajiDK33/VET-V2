Workplace Small Talk
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "It has been [adjective] lately — how are you managing [situation]?"

Pre Test Questions:

1. "It has been quite hectic lately — how are you managing the new project deadlines?"
2. "It has been very busy lately — how are you managing the extra workload?"
3. "It has been quite challenging lately — how are you managing the new system changes?"
4. "It has been rather intense lately — how are you managing the back-to-back meetings?"
5. "It has been fairly stressful lately — how are you managing the tight schedules?"
6. "It has been quite demanding lately — how are you managing the client expectations?"
7. "It has been extremely productive lately — how are you managing your time so well?"
8. "It has been a bit overwhelming lately — how are you managing the team transitions?"
9. "It has been quite exciting lately — how are you managing all the new responsibilities?"
10. "It has been pretty rushed lately — how are you managing the end-of-month targets?"

Post Test Questions:

1. "It has been very rewarding lately — how are you managing to keep up the good work?"
2. "It has been rather unpredictable lately — how are you managing the constant changes?"
3. "It has been fairly quiet lately — how are you managing your project pipeline?"
4. "It has been quite motivating lately — how are you managing to stay so focused?"
5. "It has been a little slow lately — how are you managing to stay productive?"
6. "It has been surprisingly smooth lately — how are you managing your tasks so efficiently?"
7. "It has been quite eventful lately — how are you managing all the new launches?"
8. "It has been quite fast-paced lately — how are you managing to complete everything on time?"
9. "It has been fairly collaborative lately — how are you managing to work across so many teams?"
10. "It has been very competitive lately — how are you managing the pressure from the targets?"
