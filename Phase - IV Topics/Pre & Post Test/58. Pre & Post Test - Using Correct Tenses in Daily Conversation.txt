Using Correct Tenses in Daily Conversation
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Yesterday I [past action] and today I am [present action]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Yesterday I completed the first draft and today I am proofreading the final version."
2. "Yesterday I attended the training session and today I am applying what I learned."
3. "Yesterday I submitted the project report and today I am preparing for the next assignment."
4. "Yesterday I reviewed the client feedback and today I am making the necessary changes."
5. "Yesterday I finished the market analysis and today I am preparing the summary."
6. "Yesterday I updated the sales database and today I am generating the weekly report."
7. "Yesterday I met with the client and today I am drafting the follow-up email."
8. "Yesterday I organised the team files and today I am creating the project folder."
9. "Yesterday I fixed the software issue and today I am testing the updated version."
10. "Yesterday I completed the inventory count and today I am placing the reorder request."

Post Test Questions:

1. "Yesterday I attended the board meeting and today I am working on the action points."
2. "Yesterday I sent the proposal and today I am waiting for the client's response."
3. "Yesterday I designed the presentation layout and today I am adding the final content."
4. "Yesterday I completed the employee survey and today I am analysing the results."
5. "Yesterday I repaired the broken equipment and today I am running a quality check."
6. "Yesterday I finished the onboarding checklist and today I am scheduling the induction."
7. "Yesterday I reviewed the project timeline and today I am reassigning the tasks."
8. "Yesterday I conducted the interview and today I am reviewing the candidate profiles."
9. "Yesterday I collected the data and today I am building the performance dashboard."
10. "Yesterday I completed the product demonstration and today I am following up with the leads."
