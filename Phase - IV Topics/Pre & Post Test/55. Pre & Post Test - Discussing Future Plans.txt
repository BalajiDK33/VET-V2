Discussing Future Plans
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "I am planning to [action] in [timeframe]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "I am planning to enroll in an online certification course in the next two months."
2. "I am planning to visit my hometown in December."
3. "I am planning to start a fitness routine in the new year."
4. "I am planning to apply for a promotion in the next six months."
5. "I am planning to learn a new skill in the coming quarter."
6. "I am planning to save for a vacation in the next year."
7. "I am planning to join a professional development workshop in January."
8. "I am planning to move to a new apartment in March."
9. "I am planning to complete a language course in the next three months."
10. "I am planning to start my own business in the next two years."

Post Test Questions:

1. "I am planning to read ten books in the coming year."
2. "I am planning to attend an industry conference in the next quarter."
3. "I am planning to launch a personal blog in the next month."
4. "I am planning to get a health check-up done in the coming week."
5. "I am planning to upgrade my laptop in the next few months."
6. "I am planning to volunteer at a local NGO in the coming year."
7. "I am planning to take a cooking class in January."
8. "I am planning to complete a marathon in the next six months."
9. "I am planning to reconnect with old friends in the coming holiday season."
10. "I am planning to redesign my workspace in the next few weeks."
