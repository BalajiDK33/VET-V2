Talking about Past Events
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Last [time period], I [action] and it was a great experience."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Last weekend, I visited a science exhibition and it was a great experience."
2. "Last month, I attended a team building event and it was a great experience."
3. "Last week, I participated in a cooking class and it was a great experience."
4. "Last holiday, I explored a new city with my friends and it was a great experience."
5. "Last Sunday, I volunteered at a community event and it was a great experience."
6. "Last Friday, I attended a live music concert and it was a great experience."
7. "Last summer, I completed an online course and it was a great experience."
8. "Last evening, I joined a group fitness session and it was a great experience."
9. "Last Tuesday, I visited a book fair in the city and it was a great experience."
10. "Last year, I went on a solo trip to the mountains and it was a great experience."

Post Test Questions:

1. "Last Saturday, I took part in a photography workshop and it was a great experience."
2. "Last quarter, I led a project presentation for the first time and it was a great experience."
3. "Last month, I joined a public speaking club and it was a great experience."
4. "Last week, I helped organise a charity drive at my workplace and it was a great experience."
5. "Last winter, I learned how to cook a new cuisine and it was a great experience."
6. "Last evening, I watched a documentary on wildlife and it was a great experience."
7. "Last year, I completed a professional certification and it was a great experience."
8. "Last Thursday, I attended a networking event in my field and it was a great experience."
9. "Last month, I mentored a junior colleague for the first time and it was a great experience."
10. "Last weekend, I visited a historical museum with my family and it was a great experience."
