Understanding Instructions

Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "If I understand correctly, I need to [task] by [deadline]."

1. "If I understand correctly, I need to prepare the report by Monday."
2. "If I understand correctly, I need to send the client email by end of day."
3. "If I understand correctly, I need to update the sales data by noon."
4. "If I understand correctly, I need to arrange the meeting by tomorrow morning."
5. "If I understand correctly, I need to complete the presentation by Friday."
6. "If I understand correctly, I need to submit the proposal by this evening."
7. "If I understand correctly, I need to review the document by Wednesday."
8. "If I understand correctly, I need to finalise the budget by next week."
9. "If I understand correctly, I need to call the client by three o'clock."
10. "If I understand correctly, I need to share the project update by Thursday."
