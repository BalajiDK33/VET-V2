Making Polite Requests
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Would it be possible for you to [action] by [time]?"

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Would it be possible for you to share the file by end of day?"
2. "Would it be possible for you to review my report by tomorrow morning?"
3. "Would it be possible for you to complete your task by Friday?"
4. "Would it be possible for you to approve my plan by noon?"
5. "Would it be possible for you to reschedule the meeting by this afternoon?"
6. "Would it be possible for you to send the feedback by Thursday?"
7. "Would it be possible for you to confirm the details by end of this week?"
8. "Would it be possible for you to update the spreadsheet by tomorrow?"
9. "Would it be possible for you to respond to the email by this evening?"
10. "Would it be possible for you to prepare the summary by Monday morning?"

Post Test Questions:

1. "Would it be possible for you to arrange the venue by next week?"
2. "Would it be possible for you to collect the documents by this afternoon?"
3. "Would it be possible for you to check the figures by end of day?"
4. "Would it be possible for you to forward the email by this morning?"
5. "Would it be possible for you to submit the form by Friday evening?"
6. "Would it be possible for you to provide the data by tomorrow afternoon?"
7. "Would it be possible for you to finalise the agenda by Wednesday?"
8. "Would it be possible for you to coordinate with the team by this week?"
9. "Would it be possible for you to complete the revision by next Monday?"
10. "Would it be possible for you to share your availability by end of day?"
