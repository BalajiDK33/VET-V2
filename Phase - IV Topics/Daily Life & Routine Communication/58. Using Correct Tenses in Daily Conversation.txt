Using Correct Tenses in Daily Conversation
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Yesterday I [past action] and today I am [present action]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your colleague asks what you worked on yesterday and what you are doing today. How do you respond?
2. Your manager asks about your progress from yesterday and your current task. How do you answer?
3. A friend asks what you did yesterday and what you are doing now. How do you tell them?
4. Your team leader asks for an update on your yesterday's task and today's work. How do you respond?
5. Your family member asks what you did at work yesterday and what you are doing today. How do you describe it?

Examples:

"Yesterday I completed the first draft and today I am proofreading the final version."
"Yesterday I attended the training session and today I am applying what I learned."
"Yesterday I submitted the project report and today I am preparing for the next assignment."
"Yesterday I reviewed the client feedback and today I am making the necessary changes."
