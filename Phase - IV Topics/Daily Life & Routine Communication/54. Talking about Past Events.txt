Talking about Past Events
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Last [time period], I [action] and it was a great experience."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your friend asks what you did last weekend. How do you describe a past event?
2. A colleague asks how your holiday was. How do you talk about a past experience?
3. Your family member asks what happened at your office event last week. How do you describe it?
4. A friend asks if you did anything interesting last month. How do you talk about a past activity?
5. Your colleague asks about your experience at a recent training. How do you describe it?

Examples:

"Last weekend, I visited a science exhibition and it was a great experience."
"Last month, I attended a team building event and it was a great experience."
"Last week, I participated in a cooking class and it was a great experience."
"Last holiday, I explored a new city with my friends and it was a great experience."
