Discussing Future Plans
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I am planning to [action] in [timeframe]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your friend asks what you are doing next weekend. How do you talk about your plans?
2. Your colleague asks about your career plans for the next year. How do you share your plan?
3. Your family asks what you are planning for the upcoming holiday. How do you describe your plan?
4. Your manager asks about your goals for the next three months. How do you share your plans?
5. A friend asks if you have any travel plans coming up. How do you talk about your future plans?

Examples:

"I am planning to enroll in an online certification course in the next two months."
"I am planning to visit my hometown in December."
"I am planning to start a fitness routine in the new year."
"I am planning to apply for a promotion in the next six months."
