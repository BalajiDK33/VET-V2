Talking about Chores and Everyday Activities
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Every [time], I make it a habit to [activity]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Your friend asks how you keep your home organised. How do you describe your routine?
2. A colleague asks how you stay productive at home. How do you talk about your daily habits?
3. Your family member asks how you manage your time. How do you describe your routine?
4. A friend asks what you do to stay healthy. How do you describe your everyday habits?
5. Your colleague asks how you start your day. How do you talk about your morning routine?

Examples:

"Every morning, I make it a habit to plan my tasks for the day."
"Every weekend, I make it a habit to clean and organise my workspace."
"Every evening, I make it a habit to prepare my clothes and bag for the next day."
"Every night, I make it a habit to review what I accomplished during the day."
