Handling Interruptions
Level: G0
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I understand [point], and if I may just add [response]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. A colleague interrupts you during a presentation. How do you handle the interruption politely?
2. Someone cuts in while you are explaining your idea in a meeting. How do you respond?
3. A team member interrupts your update during a discussion. How do you handle it?
4. Your manager interrupts you while you are sharing a report. How do you respond politely?
5. A colleague keeps speaking over you in a group conversation. How do you handle it?

Examples:

"I understand your concern, and if I may just add that we have a solution ready."
"I understand the deadline is tight, and if I may just add that we can split the work."
"I understand your point about the budget, and if I may just add that we have a lower-cost option."
"I understand the urgency, and if I may just add that I have already started on it."
