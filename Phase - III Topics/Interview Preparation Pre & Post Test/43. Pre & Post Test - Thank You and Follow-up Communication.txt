Concept: Interview Preparation — Thank You and Follow-up Communication
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for [role]. I look forward to [next step]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the HR executive role. I look forward to receiving your feedback."
2. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the sales executive position. I look forward to hearing from you."
3. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the marketing associate role. I look forward to the next steps."
4. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the software developer position. I look forward to your decision."
5. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the customer service role. I look forward to your update."
6. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the finance analyst position. I look forward to your response."
7. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the content writer role. I look forward to hearing from your team."
8. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the operations manager position. I look forward to the selection outcome."
9. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the business development role. I look forward to your confirmation."
10. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the data analyst position. I look forward to the next round."

Post Test Questions:

1. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the team leader role. I look forward to your valuable feedback."
2. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the project coordinator position. I look forward to hearing from you soon."
3. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the graphic designer role. I look forward to your decision."
4. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the logistics executive position. I look forward to the offer letter."
5. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the product manager role. I look forward to the next steps in the process."
6. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the public relations executive position. I look forward to your feedback."
7. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the training coordinator role. I look forward to joining your team."
8. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the quality analyst position. I look forward to your update."
9. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the branch manager role. I look forward to a positive response."
10. "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the digital marketing executive position. I look forward to hearing from you."
