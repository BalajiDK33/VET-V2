Concept: Interview Preparation — Presenting Career Goals
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "My career goal is to [goal], and I plan to achieve it by [action]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "My career goal is to become a team leader, and I plan to achieve it by improving my management skills."
2. "My career goal is to work in a multinational company, and I plan to achieve it by building my communication skills."
3. "My career goal is to become a software engineer, and I plan to achieve it by completing relevant certifications."
4. "My career goal is to start my own business, and I plan to achieve it by gaining industry experience first."
5. "My career goal is to become an HR manager, and I plan to achieve it by studying human resource management."
6. "My career goal is to work in digital marketing, and I plan to achieve it by learning online marketing tools."
7. "My career goal is to become a financial analyst, and I plan to achieve it by completing a finance certification."
8. "My career goal is to lead a sales team, and I plan to achieve it by building strong client relationships."
9. "My career goal is to become a product manager, and I plan to achieve it by gaining cross-functional experience."
10. "My career goal is to work in data analytics, and I plan to achieve it by learning data tools and programming."

Post Test Questions:

1. "My career goal is to become a business consultant, and I plan to achieve it by developing problem-solving skills."
2. "My career goal is to work in operations management, and I plan to achieve it by improving my planning skills."
3. "My career goal is to become a trainer, and I plan to achieve it by building my presentation and teaching skills."
4. "My career goal is to work in customer success, and I plan to achieve it by strengthening my interpersonal skills."
5. "My career goal is to become a project manager, and I plan to achieve it by earning a project management certification."
6. "My career goal is to work in content writing, and I plan to achieve it by practising writing every day."
7. "My career goal is to become a graphic designer, and I plan to achieve it by completing a design course."
8. "My career goal is to work in supply chain management, and I plan to achieve it by learning logistics processes."
9. "My career goal is to become a team manager, and I plan to achieve it by developing my leadership and communication skills."
10. "My career goal is to work in public relations, and I plan to achieve it by building my networking and communication skills."
