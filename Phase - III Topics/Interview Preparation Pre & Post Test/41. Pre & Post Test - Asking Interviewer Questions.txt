Concept: Interview Preparation — Asking Interviewer Questions
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "Could you tell me more about [topic]?"

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Could you tell me more about the team I will be working with?"
2. "Could you tell me more about the daily responsibilities of this role?"
3. "Could you tell me more about the growth opportunities in this company?"
4. "Could you tell me more about the work culture and environment here?"
5. "Could you tell me more about the training and onboarding process?"
6. "Could you tell me more about the performance review process?"
7. "Could you tell me more about the expectations for this role in the first three months?"
8. "Could you tell me more about the technologies and tools used in this role?"
9. "Could you tell me more about the reporting structure in this team?"
10. "Could you tell me more about the company's vision and future plans?"

Post Test Questions:

1. "Could you tell me more about the key challenges of this position?"
2. "Could you tell me more about the opportunities for skill development here?"
3. "Could you tell me more about the work-life balance in this organisation?"
4. "Could you tell me more about the next steps in the selection process?"
5. "Could you tell me more about the projects I will be handling in this role?"
6. "Could you tell me more about the collaboration between different teams?"
7. "Could you tell me more about the company's approach to employee feedback?"
8. "Could you tell me more about the typical career path from this position?"
9. "Could you tell me more about the joining date and formalities?"
10. "Could you tell me more about the support available for new employees?"
