Concept: Interview Preparation — Professional Email Writing
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "I am writing to [purpose] and I look forward to [response]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "I am writing to apply for the job opening and I look forward to your response."
2. "I am writing to follow up on my application and I look forward to an update."
3. "I am writing to request an interview appointment and I look forward to your confirmation."
4. "I am writing to ask for feedback on my interview and I look forward to your response."
5. "I am writing to confirm my interview schedule and I look forward to your reply."
6. "I am writing to express my interest in the role and I look forward to hearing from you."
7. "I am writing to submit my resume for consideration and I look forward to your feedback."
8. "I am writing to enquire about the job vacancy and I look forward to your guidance."
9. "I am writing to thank you for the interview opportunity and I look forward to the next steps."
10. "I am writing to share my updated resume and I look forward to your review."

Post Test Questions:

1. "I am writing to reschedule my interview and I look forward to your convenience."
2. "I am writing to introduce myself as a candidate and I look forward to a discussion."
3. "I am writing to request a reference letter and I look forward to your support."
4. "I am writing to notify you of my acceptance of the offer and I look forward to joining."
5. "I am writing to ask about the selection status and I look forward to your update."
6. "I am writing to share my portfolio for your review and I look forward to your feedback."
7. "I am writing to request a meeting to discuss my application and I look forward to your response."
8. "I am writing to clarify my interview details and I look forward to your confirmation."
9. "I am writing to follow up on the job offer and I look forward to your decision."
10. "I am writing to withdraw my application and I look forward to future opportunities with your organisation."
