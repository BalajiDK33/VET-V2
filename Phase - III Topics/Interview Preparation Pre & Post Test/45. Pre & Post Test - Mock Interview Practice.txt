Concept: Interview Preparation — Mock Interview Practice
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure: "My greatest strength is [skill] because [reason]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "My greatest strength is communication because I can express my ideas clearly."
2. "My greatest strength is teamwork because I work well with people from different backgrounds."
3. "My greatest strength is problem-solving because I stay calm and find solutions under pressure."
4. "My greatest strength is time management because I always complete tasks before the deadline."
5. "My greatest strength is adaptability because I can quickly adjust to new environments and changes."
6. "My greatest strength is leadership because I can guide and motivate my team effectively."
7. "My greatest strength is attention to detail because I always ensure accuracy in my work."
8. "My greatest strength is creativity because I come up with fresh and practical ideas."
9. "My greatest strength is patience because I handle difficult situations without losing my composure."
10. "My greatest strength is decision-making because I can analyse situations and choose the best course of action."

Post Test Questions:

1. "My greatest strength is organisation because I can manage multiple tasks efficiently."
2. "My greatest strength is critical thinking because I evaluate problems before jumping to conclusions."
3. "My greatest strength is empathy because I understand and consider the feelings of my colleagues."
4. "My greatest strength is positivity because I maintain a motivated attitude even during challenges."
5. "My greatest strength is listening because I pay close attention to understand others better."
6. "My greatest strength is initiative because I take action without waiting to be told."
7. "My greatest strength is reliability because my team can always count on me to deliver results."
8. "My greatest strength is negotiation because I can find solutions that work for all parties."
9. "My greatest strength is multitasking because I can handle several responsibilities at the same time."
10. "My greatest strength is confidence because I present my ideas and opinions clearly and assertively."
