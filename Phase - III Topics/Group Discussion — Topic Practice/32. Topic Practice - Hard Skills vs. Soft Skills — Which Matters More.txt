Concept: Group Discussion — Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Priya says hard skills are more important than soft skills. Share your view using Structure 1.
2. Rahul says soft skills help in teamwork and communication. Respond using Structure 1.
3. Ram says technical knowledge is the only thing employers look for. Share your view using Structure 2.
4. Sneha says soft skills help in building better relationships at work. Respond using Structure 1.
5. Arjun says hard skills alone cannot make you successful. Share your view using Structure 2.

Examples:

"Hard skills offer technical ability for better job performance."
"Soft skills offer better teamwork and communication in the workplace."
"While hard skills offer technical expertise, it also has a limitation of poor interpersonal skills."
"While soft skills offer better communication, it also has a lack of technical knowledge."
