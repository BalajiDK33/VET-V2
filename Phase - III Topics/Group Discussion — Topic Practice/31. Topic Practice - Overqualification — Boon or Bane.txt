Concept: Group Discussion — Overqualification — Boon or Bane
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Priya says overqualification is a problem for employers. Share your view using Structure 1.
2. Rahul says overqualified candidates always demand higher pay. Respond using Structure 2.
3. Ram says hiring overqualified people improves team performance. Agree and add a reason using Structure 1.
4. Sneha says overqualified employees leave jobs quickly. Respond using Structure 2.
5. Arjun says overqualification helps the organization grow faster. Share your view using Structure 1.

Examples:

"Overqualification offers a higher level of expertise to the organization."
"Overqualification offers better problem-solving ability to the team."
"While overqualification offers advanced skills, it also has a risk of higher salary demands."
"While overqualification offers expertise, it also has the challenge of employee dissatisfaction."
