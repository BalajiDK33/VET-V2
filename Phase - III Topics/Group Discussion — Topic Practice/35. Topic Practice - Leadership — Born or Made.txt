Concept: Group Discussion — Leadership — Born or Made
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. What does born leadership offer to a team?
2. While born leadership offers natural talent, what challenge does it also bring?
3. What does learned leadership offer to an organization?
4. While learned leadership offers adaptability, what risk does it also bring?
5. What does strong leadership offer to a company's growth?

Examples:

"Born leadership offers natural confidence and strong decision-making ability."
"Learned leadership offers adaptability and growth through experience."
"While born leadership offers natural talent, it also has a risk of being inflexible."
"While learned leadership offers skill development, it also has the need for constant training."
