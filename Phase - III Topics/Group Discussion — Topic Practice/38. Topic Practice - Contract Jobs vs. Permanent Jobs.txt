Concept: Group Discussion — Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Priya says contract jobs are better because they offer flexibility. Share your view using Structure 1.
2. Rahul says permanent jobs are better because they offer stability. Respond using Structure 1.
3. Ram says contract jobs do not offer job security. Respond using Structure 2.
4. Sneha says permanent jobs limit your exposure to different industries. Share your view using Structure 2.
5. Arjun says contract jobs are becoming more popular in the modern workplace. Share your view using Structure 1.

Examples:

"Contract jobs offer flexibility and diverse work experience."
"Permanent jobs offer stability and long-term career growth."
"While contract jobs offer flexibility, it also has a lack of job security."
"While permanent jobs offer stability, it also has limited variety in work."
