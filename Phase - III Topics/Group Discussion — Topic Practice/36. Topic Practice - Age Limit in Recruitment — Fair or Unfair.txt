Concept: Group Discussion — Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. What does an age limit in recruitment offer to a company? Use Structure 1.
2. While an age limit offers a younger workforce, what challenge does it also bring? Respond using Structure 2.
3. What does no age limit in recruitment offer to an organization? Use Structure 1.
4. While no age limit offers diversity, what risk does it also bring? Give your answer using Structure 2.
5. What does an age limit in recruitment offer to team energy? Use Structure 1.

Examples:

"Age limit in recruitment offers a younger and energetic workforce."
"No age limit in recruitment offers diversity and experience to the organization."
"While age limit in recruitment offers fresh talent, it also has a loss of experienced professionals."
"While no age limit offers experience, it also has a higher cost of employment."
