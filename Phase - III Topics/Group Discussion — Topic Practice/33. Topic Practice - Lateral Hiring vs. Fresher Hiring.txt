Concept: Group Discussion — Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Overqualification — Boon or Bane, Hard Skills vs. Soft Skills — Which Matters More, Lateral Hiring vs. Fresher Hiring, Resume — Does It Truly Reflect a Candidate, Leadership — Born or Made, Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair, Work Pressure vs. Work-Life Balance, Contract Jobs vs. Permanent Jobs

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. Priya says lateral hiring is better because candidates come with experience. Share your view using Structure 1.
2. Rahul says fresher hiring is better because freshers are easy to train. Respond using Structure 1.
3. Ram says lateral hires always demand higher salaries. Respond using Structure 2.
4. Sneha says freshers take too long to become productive. Respond using Structure 2.
5. Arjun says companies should prefer lateral hiring for senior positions. Share your view using Structure 1.

Examples:

"Lateral hiring offers immediate expertise to the organization."
"Fresher hiring offers new energy and fresh perspectives to the team."
"While lateral hiring offers experience, it also has a higher cost of recruitment."
"While fresher hiring offers adaptability, it also has a longer training period."
