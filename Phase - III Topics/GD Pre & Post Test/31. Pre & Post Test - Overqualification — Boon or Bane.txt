Concept: Group Discussion — Overqualification — Boon or Bane
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Overqualification offers a higher level of expertise to the organization."
2. "While overqualification offers advanced skills, it also has a risk of higher salary demands."
3. "Overqualification offers better problem-solving ability to the team."
4. "While overqualification offers expertise, it also has the challenge of employee dissatisfaction."
5. "Overqualification offers faster completion of complex tasks."
6. "While overqualification offers better performance, it also has a risk of the employee leaving soon."
7. "Overqualification offers strong analytical skills to the workplace."
8. "While overqualification offers technical strength, it also has a risk of the candidate feeling undervalued."
9. "Overqualification offers a more knowledgeable and skilled workforce."
10. "While overqualification offers industry experience, it also has the challenge of fitting into a junior role."

Post Test Questions:

1. "Overqualification offers better quality output in less time."
2. "While overqualification offers high productivity, it also has a risk of low job satisfaction."
3. "Overqualification offers a competitive advantage to the company."
4. "While overqualification offers strong knowledge, it also has the drawback of limited career growth in the role."
5. "Overqualification offers deeper understanding of the industry."
6. "While overqualification offers quick learning, it also has a risk of the employee getting bored easily."
7. "Overqualification offers higher efficiency in handling difficult tasks."
8. "While overqualification offers great skills, it also has the challenge of matching salary expectations."
9. "Overqualification offers valuable mentoring ability to junior employees."
10. "While overqualification offers efficiency, it also has a risk of creating team imbalance."
