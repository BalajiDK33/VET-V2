Concept: Group Discussion — Age Limit in Recruitment — Fair or Unfair
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do

Sentence Structure 1: "[Topic] offers [advantage]."
Sentence Structure 2: "While [topic] offers [advantage], it also has a [drawback]."

Pre Test Questions:

1. "Age limit in recruitment offers a younger and energetic workforce."
2. "While age limit in recruitment offers fresh talent, it also has a loss of experienced professionals."
3. "Age limit in recruitment offers better adaptability to new technology."
4. "While age limit in recruitment offers younger candidates, it also has a risk of limited expertise."
5. "Age limit in recruitment offers candidates who are open to learning."
6. "While age limit in recruitment offers cost savings, it also has a challenge of high training needs."
7. "Age limit in recruitment offers a long-term employment period for the company."
8. "While age limit in recruitment offers energy, it also has a risk of less industry knowledge."
9. "Age limit in recruitment offers lower salary expectations from younger candidates."
10. "While age limit in recruitment offers adaptability, it also has a drawback of fewer mentors in the team."

1. "No age limit in recruitment offers diversity and experience to the organization."
2. "While no age limit offers experience, it also has a higher cost of employment."
3. "No age limit in recruitment offers a wider pool of skilled candidates."
4. "While no age limit offers diversity, it also has a challenge of managing different generations."
5. "No age limit in recruitment offers the experience needed for complex roles."
6. "While no age limit offers expertise, it also has a risk of higher salary demands."
7. "No age limit in recruitment offers equal opportunities for all professionals."
8. "While no age limit offers a wider talent pool, it also has a drawback of slower technology adoption."
9. "No age limit in recruitment offers a mix of young and experienced talent."
10. "While no age limit offers inclusion, it also has a challenge of varied expectations and work styles."
