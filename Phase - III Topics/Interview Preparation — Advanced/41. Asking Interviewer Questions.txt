Concept: Interview Preparation — Asking Interviewer Questions
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Could you tell me more about [topic]?"

Roleplay Questions:

1. The interviewer asks if you have any questions. Ask about the team you will work with.
2. The interviewer asks if you want to know anything about the role. Ask about daily responsibilities.
3. The interviewer says you can ask any question you have. Ask about growth opportunities.
4. The interviewer invites your questions at the end of the interview. Ask about the work culture.
5. The interviewer gives you a chance to ask about the company. Ask about training and support.

Examples:

"Could you tell me more about the team I will be working with?"
"Could you tell me more about the daily responsibilities of this role?"
"Could you tell me more about the growth opportunities in this company?"
"Could you tell me more about the work culture and environment here?"
