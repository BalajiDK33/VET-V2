Concept: Interview Preparation — Mock Interview Practice
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "My greatest strength is [skill] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. The interviewer asks about your greatest strength. Share your strength and reason.
2. The interviewer asks what skill makes you stand out. Share your strength and reason.
3. The interviewer asks how your strength helps you at work. Share your skill and reason.
4. The interviewer asks you to describe one quality that defines you. Share your strength and reason.
5. The interviewer asks what you are best at. Share your greatest strength and reason.

Examples:

"My greatest strength is communication because I can express my ideas clearly."
"My greatest strength is teamwork because I work well with people from different backgrounds."
"My greatest strength is problem-solving because I stay calm and find solutions under pressure."
"My greatest strength is time management because I always complete tasks before the deadline."
