Concept: Interview Preparation — Presenting Ideas Clearly and Confidently
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "To begin with [point] and in conclusion [summary]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. The interviewer asks your opinion on remote work. Present your idea clearly using the structure.
2. The interviewer asks how you would improve team productivity. Present your idea using the structure.
3. The interviewer asks for your view on digital marketing. Share your idea using the structure.
4. The interviewer asks how you handle work pressure. Present your point using the structure.
5. The interviewer asks your opinion on teamwork in the workplace. Share your view using the structure.

Examples:

"To begin with remote work saves commute time and in conclusion it improves overall productivity."
"To begin with teamwork builds trust and in conclusion it leads to better results."
"To begin with digital marketing is cost-effective and in conclusion it reaches a wider audience."
"To begin with regular breaks reduce stress and in conclusion they improve focus and efficiency."
