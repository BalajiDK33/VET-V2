Concept: Interview Preparation — Professional Email Writing
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "I am writing to [purpose] and I look forward to [response]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. You want to apply for a job opening. Write the opening line of your email.
2. You want to follow up on your job application. Write the opening line of your email.
3. You want to request an interview appointment. Write the opening line of your email.
4. You want to ask for feedback after an interview. Write the opening line of your email.
5. You want to confirm your interview schedule. Write the opening line of your email.

Examples:

"I am writing to apply for the job opening and I look forward to your response."
"I am writing to follow up on my application and I look forward to an update."
"I am writing to request an interview appointment and I look forward to your confirmation."
"I am writing to ask for feedback on my interview and I look forward to your response."
