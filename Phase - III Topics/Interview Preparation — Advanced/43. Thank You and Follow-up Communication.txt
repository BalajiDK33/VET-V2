Concept: Interview Preparation — Thank You and Follow-up Communication
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for [role]. I look forward to [next step]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. You have completed an interview for a sales executive role. Write your thank you message.
2. You have finished an interview for a marketing associate position. Write your follow-up message.
3. You attended an interview for an HR executive role. Write your thank you note.
4. You completed a technical interview for a software developer position. Write your message.
5. You had an interview for a customer service role. Write your thank you and follow-up message.

Examples:

"Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the HR executive role. I look forward to receiving your feedback."
"Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the sales executive position. I look forward to hearing from you."
"Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the marketing associate role. I look forward to the next steps."
"Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the software developer position. I look forward to your decision."
