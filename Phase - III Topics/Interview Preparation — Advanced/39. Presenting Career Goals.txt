Concept: Interview Preparation — Presenting Career Goals
Level: A1
Lesson Type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence Structure: "My career goal is to [goal], and I plan to achieve it by [action]."

Roleplay Questions:

1. The interviewer asks about your career goal in management. Share your goal and plan.
2. The interviewer asks where you see yourself in five years. Share your career goal and action plan.
3. The interviewer asks how you plan to grow in this industry. Share your goal and steps to achieve it.
4. The interviewer asks what motivates you to work in this field. Share your career goal and plan.
5. The interviewer asks about your long-term professional ambition. Share your goal and how you plan to achieve it.

Examples:

"My career goal is to become a team leader, and I plan to achieve it by improving my management skills."
"My career goal is to work in a multinational company, and I plan to achieve it by building my communication skills."
"My career goal is to become a software engineer, and I plan to achieve it by completing relevant certifications."
"My career goal is to start my own business, and I plan to achieve it by gaining industry experience first."
